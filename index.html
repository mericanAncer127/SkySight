SkySight Roof Report™

Our Roof Reports™ use the most up-to-date aerial imagery available. We use cutting edge Machine Learning techniques to deliver accurate and consistent roof measurements.

Each report contains an Area Diagram, Length Diagram, Pitch Diagram, Orthographic Views, and a Summary.

Download Sample Roof Report™

Area Diagram

Areas for each roof facet. Areas are calculated taking into account the pitch and 3D position of the roof.

Length Diagram

Length of all roof lines. Different colors are used to denote Ridges, Hips, Valleys, Rakes, and Eaves.

Pitch Diagram

Roof pitches labeled on roof facets. Pitch values are given as a single number and represent the "rise" for a 12 inch "run" (e.g. 8 represents a pitch of 8/12)

Orthographic Views

Views of the address from North, South, East, and West directions.

Summary

The summary section of the Roof Report provides the roof areas for each pitch, waste calculations and required squares for varying percentages, and lengths of different roof line types.
